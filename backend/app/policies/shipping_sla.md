# Shipping & Delivery Service Levels

## Order Processing / Fulfillment (SHIP-FUL-01)
- **Cut-off Time:** Orders placed before **2:00 PM EST** process the same business day.
- **After Cut-off:** Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST process the next business day.
- **Peak Season:** During Black Friday/Cyber Monday, processing may take up to **72 hours** before shipment.

## Lost in Transit (SHIP-LOST-02)
If a shipment is reported as missing:

### Tracking stuck on "Label Created" (SHIP-LOST-02A)
- If tracking remains **Label Created** for more than **5 business days**, a replacement may be issued.

### In-Transit with no scans (SHIP-LOST-02B)
- A package is not considered lost until **10 days** have passed since the last carrier scan.

### Marked Delivered but Not Received (SHIP-LOST-02C)
- Ask the customer to wait **48 hours** (carriers may pre-scan deliveries).
- Ask the customer to check with neighbors/building management.
- If still missing after 48 hours:
  - open a **Carrier Investigation**
  - do not issue a refund immediately

## Address Corrections (SHIP-ADDR-03)
- **Window:** Address updates are allowed within **60 minutes** of purchase.
- **After Window:** The order is locked for fulfillment and cannot be rerouted.
- In this case, the customer must wait for a **Return to Sender** event before any return/refund processing can begin.
