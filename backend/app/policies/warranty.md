# Warranty & Quality Guarantee

## Coverage Period (WAR-TIME-01)
- **Apparel:** **90 days** from Delivery Date.
- **Footwear & Accessories:** **180 days** from Delivery Date.

## Covered Defects (WAR-DEF-02)
Manufacturing defects include:
- **Seams:** unraveling stitching or popped seams not caused by incorrect sizing or misuse.
- **Hardware:** broken zippers, snaps, buttons, or clasps under normal use.
- **Fabric:** significant pilling within **30 days**, or color bleeding after following care instructions.

## Not Covered (WAR-EXCL-03)
The warranty does not cover:
- **Normal wear and tear:** scuff marks, fading due to extended use.
- **Accidental damage:** rips from snagging, pet damage, spills.
- **Improper care:** shrinkage or damage caused by ignoring care instructions (example: machine drying an item marked “hang dry only”).

## Claim Submission Requirements (WAR-CLAIM-04)
A warranty claim must include:
- **Order ID**
- **SKU (or item name)**
- **Description of the defect**
- **Photo evidence:** a clear photo of the defect is required before approval

## Resolution Rules (WAR-RES-05)
If a claim is approved:
1. **Replacement (preferred):** send a replacement of the exact SKU when in stock.
2. **Refund:** if the exact SKU is out of stock, issue a refund (see `refunds.md`, `REF-METH-02`).
3. **Return waiver:** in most approved defect cases, the item does not need to be returned; the customer may donate or discard it.
